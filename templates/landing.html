How it works

01

Calibrate

Sentinel learns each product's normal (error tolerance, bias, noise) from a clean slice of history. Every threshold is per-SKU, never one-size-fits-all.

02

Monitor

Eight statistical checks run weekly on every product: accuracy bands, bias, distribution drift (PSI/KS), sequential drift (Page-Hinkley), frozen feeds, outages, unit breaks.

03

Diagnose

Data bug or real demand shift? One needs a pipeline fix, the other a retrain, and every exception says which, in plain language.

04

Act

Exceptions ranked by revenue at risk, each with a recommended next action. Human attention goes where the money is.
